❤️ Dear Best Friend ❤️

Tum sirf meri friend nahi ho, tum meri life ki sabse achi aur trusted dost ho 🌸

Tumhare saath baat karna, hasna, sab kuch itna natural lagta hai, jaise hum dono ek dusre ko bina bole hi samajh lete hain 💫

Hamari dosti simple hai, lekin bahut special hai 💖 Isme respect hai, trust hai, aur ek strong connection hai

Mujhe accha lagta hai ki tum meri life ka hissa ho 😊 Aur main hamesha chahta hoon ki hum dono aise hi ache dost bane rahe 💯

Thank you hamesha saath dene ke liye 🙏 Tum jaisi dost milna sach me lucky baat hai ❤️✨

— Tumhara Dost
